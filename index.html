TELL ME ABOUT YOU

My story is simple. It was less than 6 months ago when I first dived in the vast world of front-end development, learning HTML & CSS, jQuery, Bootstrap and the basics of Javascript.

Since then, I've created more than 20 personal projects, and I know this is just the beginning.

My passion is cycling and coding (unfortunately not in the same time, altough I think it would be fun).

SO WHAT DO YOU DO?

I currently work as a freelancer on various platforms such as upwork, freelancer or fiverr, creating front-end solutions.

I'm specialized in HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, jQuery, Javascript and on the front end part of WordPress.
